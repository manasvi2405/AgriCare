Privacy Policy

At AgriCare, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the information you provide when using our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

When you use AgriCare, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, phone number, email address, and delivery address when placing orders.
  • Account Information: Login details and account preferences.
  • Order Information: Products purchased such as seeds, fertilizers, and other agricultural products.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our website and services.
  • Images or Data: Photos uploaded for plant disease detection or crop analysis.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information collected may be used for the following purposes:

  • To process and deliver orders.
  • To provide crop recommendations and disease detection services.
  • To improve our website and farming tools.
  • To communicate with users regarding orders, updates, and support.
  • To prevent fraud and ensure platform security.

3. Crop Recommendation and Disease Detection Data

If you upload plant images or farming data for disease detection or crop recommendations, AgriCare may process this information to provide accurate results and improve the system. This data will only be used for agricultural analysis and service improvement.

4. Sharing of Information

AgriCare does not sell or rent personal information to third parties. However, we may share information with trusted partners such as:

  • Delivery partners for shipping products.
  • Payment gateway providers for secure transactions.
  • Technical partners that help operate our platform.

5. Data Security

AgriCare uses appropriate technical and security measures to protect user information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no online platform can guarantee complete security.

6. Cookies and Website Tracking

Our website may use cookies to improve user experience, remember preferences, and analyze website traffic. Users can disable cookies through their browser settings.

7. User Rights

Users have the right to:

  • Access their personal information.
  • Request correction of incorrect information.
  • Request deletion of their account data.

8. Third-Party Links

The website may contain links to third-party websites for agricultural resources or services. AgriCare is not responsible for the privacy practices of those external websites.

9. Policy Updates

AgriCare may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect improvements or legal requirements. Updated versions will be posted on this page.
